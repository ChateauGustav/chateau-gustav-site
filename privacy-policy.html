Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Château Gustav ("we," "our," or "us") provides a mobile and web application that offers wine and food pairing recommendations, wine label scanning, and related sommelier services (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described here. If you don't agree, please don't use the App.

1. Information We Collect

Account information. If you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and Google account profile photo (if available) from Google. If you use email sign-in, we collect the email address you provide. You can also use the App as a guest without creating an account, in which case no account information is collected.

Wine label photos. When you use the label scanner, the photo you take is sent to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis (to identify the wine and generate tasting information). We store the resulting wine data (name, region, tasting notes, etc.) associated with your account if you're signed in. We do not permanently store the photo itself beyond what's needed to process the scan.

Scanned wine data. If you're signed in, wines you scan are saved to our database (via Google Firebase/Firestore) so they're available across your devices. If you're using the App as a guest, this data stays only on your device.

Saved pairings and preferences. Food/wine pairings you save, your taste preferences, and app settings (like temperature units) are currently stored only on your device, whether or not you're signed in.

Push notification tokens. If you enable notifications, we store a device token (provided by Google Firebase Cloud Messaging) so we can send you alerts — for example, when a wine you've scanned is entering its ideal drinking window.

Usage and diagnostic information. We may collect basic technical information (such as app version, device type, and crash data) to help us maintain and improve the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the App's core features: wine identification, food pairing recommendations, and the "Ask Gustav" chat assistant
  • To sync your scanned wines and account information across your devices
  • To send you notifications you've opted into, such as drinking-window alerts
  • To maintain, secure, and improve the App

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data for advertising, and the App does not display third-party ads.

3. Third-Party Services

We rely on the following third-party services to operate the App. Each has its own privacy practices, governing how they handle data on our behalf:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud Messaging) — for sign-in, data storage, and push notifications
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — for analyzing wine label photos and powering pairing recommendations and chat
  • Vercel — for hosting our backend services

We do not control these third parties' data practices beyond the data we choose to send them, and encourage you to review their own privacy policies for more detail.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your account data and scanned wine history for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at chateaugustav@gmail.com. Signing out of the App clears certain personal data (like your name and cloud-synced scans) from that device, but does not delete your account or cloud data — only account deletion does that.

5. Children's Privacy

Château Gustav is a wine and alcohol-related service intended for adults of legal drinking age in their jurisdiction. The App is not directed at, and should not be used by, anyone under 21 (or the legal drinking age where you live, if higher). We do not knowingly collect information from children.

6. Your Choices

  • You can use the App as a guest without providing any account information
  • You can disable push notifications at any time in your device settings or in-app Settings
  • You can sign out at any time, and request full account deletion by contacting us

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections and access-controlled cloud storage. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we can't guarantee absolute security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll update the "Last updated" date above when we do. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at chateaugustav@gmail.com.
