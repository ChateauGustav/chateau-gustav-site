The experience

More than a sip

At Château Gustav, we believe the joy of wine goes beyond the glass — it's about creating an immersive, comfortable experience for every guest. We craft memorable journeys where you feel at ease to explore and ask questions, in an atmosphere of shared curiosity and learning.

Our mission is to be more than a venue for wine appreciation. We're dedicated educators, passionate about unraveling the intricacies of the world of wine — from novices to enthusiasts, sharing knowledge you can carry forward long after the last pour.

It's a haven where curiosity is met with expertise, and every question is an opportunity to deepen your appreciation. Join us as we uncork the world of wine, one memorable tasting at a time.

Your host

Meet Dele

My name is Dele Faulkner, and I'm excited to share my passion for wine with you. How did I get into it? Oddly enough, it started with a glass of Macallan 18 in 2018. I wanted to elevate my palate, and scotch was a spirit I knew well.

Then, at a restaurant in Dallas, I overheard a guest speaking with the resident sommelier — someone whose profession was in a completely different industry, yet who spoke about wine with real depth. It sparked a desire: how rewarding would it be to know a passion that intimately?

Fast forward five years — a degree in Biology, time in private wealth management, and a WSET Level 2 certification — and the hobby became a clear vision: to communicate my passion for wine in a personable, relatable way, so that whether or not you drink wine, you leave with something.

— Dele Faulkner, Founder

Good to know

Frequently asked

What sets Château Gustav's tastings apart?
Our private tastings emphasize a comfortable, educational experience. We focus on a relaxed atmosphere where guests feel encouraged to ask questions and actively engage in learning about wine.
Are tastings suitable for beginners and enthusiasts alike?
Absolutely. Our tastings cater to all levels of wine knowledge. Whether you're brand new to wine or a seasoned enthusiast, we tailor the experience to your group's interests and preferences.
Can I customize the wine selection for my event?
Yes — customization is a key feature of our private tastings. We work closely with you to curate a selection that aligns with your group's preferences for a personalized, memorable experience.
Can you host at my location, or only at a set venue?
Our tastings are versatile. We can host at your preferred location, though most tastings are held at a venue of our choosing. We'll find what works best for your group.
What will I learn at a tasting?
Our goal is to deepen your understanding of wine — varietals, winemaking processes, and the regions wines come from. You'll leave with valuable insights that enhance your overall wine appreciation.
